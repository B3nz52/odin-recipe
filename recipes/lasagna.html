This is a delicious cheesy lasagna that feeds 8 and take 45 minutes to cook

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Recipe

  1. Chop onion, garlic, tomatoes
  2. Add chopped vegetables and meat to a pan to fry
  3. Cook for 10 minutes
  4. Place a layer of lasagna sheets at the bottom of a baking tray
  5. add cooked meat and vegetable
  6. repeat this process until you have used all you meat and vegetable mix and lasagna sheets
  7. bake in the oven for 45 minutes
  8. remove and serve
